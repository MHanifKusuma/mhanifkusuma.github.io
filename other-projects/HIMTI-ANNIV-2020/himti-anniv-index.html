HIMTI Anniversary 2020

"For A Year Full of Innovations Passed with Togetherness"

This year celebration aims to appreciate all the innovations created in HIMTI within the past year. The COVID-19 pandemics have brought hard times to everyone. But, HIMTI members never give in to the situation and always came up with something extraordinary. That's why I, as the chairman of HIMTI Anniversary 2020, wanted to create a night where we can leave our duties behind for a few hours and have a really fun time with each other to reward our hard work.
